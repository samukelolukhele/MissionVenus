THE NATURAL BENEFITS

Gravity

Venus has a slightly lighter gravity than Earth. This has many benefits like reducing the health issues that occur in

comparatively low gravity to Earth’s. A higher gravity also means we can retain heavier gases that are important to our survival.

Magnetic Field

Although Venus’ magnetic field is ten times smaller than Earth’s. It still makes it one of the better

candidates for colonization due to other candidate planets having a far smaller one. With a weak magnetic field we are exposed to much of the radiation that is in space.

HEAD IN THE CLOUDS

The first part of our mission is to build floating colonies within in the dense cloud systems of Venus. These cloud systems host almost comfortable temperatures.

These clouds contain gases which are toxic to us but are quite hospitable to other lifeforms like certain microbes and fauna which can support us in supplying food and crucial gases.

HARD SURFACE

The second phase of our mission is to terraform the surface of Venus. We can do this by reducing the greenhouse effect on the planet. Using certain species of microbes or even using hydrogen bombs.

There are many theorized methods we can approach this but the method we are currently laser focusing on is using microbes in which can convert CO2 in oxygen.
